Our apostle recontextualizes Isaiah’s discourse in order to associ-

Whereas the concept of foolishness turns out to be antithetical to this wis-

dom, Paul presents a reverse ordering in which proclamation of the cruci-

ed Messiah, perceived by the wise in this world as foolishness, has become

latter is foolish in comparison.
