JOHN 18

Arrest and Trials Faced by Jesus

Jesus’ passion and resurrection (18:1—20:31)

John 18:1—20:31 constitutes one unit, since it witnesses to the conjoined

events of Jesus’ passion and resurrection. However, two full chapters

(18:1—19:42) are devoted to the passion narrative.
