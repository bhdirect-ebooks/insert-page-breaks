Justice and Peace

Related to the concern about dignity, Paul also seeks to handle the inevi-

table question that comes to those who suer—is God fair (theodicy)? Can

God be trusted to do what it right? Why does he keep silent and invisible?

What have I done such that he won’t rescue me?

Paul responds to the essalonians’ suering by pointing to a future

Suering and wrath are not ultimate things, they are penultimate things.

Paul’s ultimate interest in peace (eirēnē) is demon-
