God’s Call to Holiness (4:1–8)

Paul begins this section by reminding the essalonians (through the au-

thority of the Lord Jesus) about the teaching that they had already received

from him (4:1). When he originally instructed them, he shared not simply

the good-news message, but also counsel regarding “how you must walk

and live to please God.” We sometimes call this “ethics,” but Paul would not

have seen a distinction between “theology” and “ethics.”
