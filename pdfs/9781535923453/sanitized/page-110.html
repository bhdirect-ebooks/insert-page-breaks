As for some today, Irene faced the morose reality that loved ones die.

She tries to maintain a positive spirit by encouraging her friends to take

comfort. And yet there is no clear referent of that hope. But for Paul it was

not this way. Yes, of course people must grieve. But believers face the loss

knowing the impending work of God to wipe away every tear and quiet

mourning and shouts of agony (Rev 7:17; 21:4).
