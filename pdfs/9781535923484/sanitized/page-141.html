If then, according to Aristotle, humans are the only animals (zoon)

evil, and to distinguish between justice and injustice, then 2 Peter’s charac-

terization of the false-teachers as irrational beasts (alogon zoon), worthy

only of slaughter (eis alosin kai phthoran), is to remove them completely

from the realm of that which is human.
