Mercy, was added to the greeting in v. 1 to make Jude’s use of

the generic epistolary salutation more distinct. But it also may emphasize

the eschatological nature of his overall message. Here, in v. 21, mercy ap-

two are very likely connected, it has also been suggested that the occurrence

of mercy here may be more closely derived from 1 Enoch 1:8.
