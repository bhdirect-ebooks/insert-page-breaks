Authorship of Pastoral Letters

1 Timothy, 2 Timothy, and Titus begin: “Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus,”

or “Paul, a slave of God, but apostle of Jesus Christ.” Because of similarity

to the headings to Paul’s other letters, many commentators conclude that

Paul is the author of all the Pastorals.

2

Even commentators, such as C. K.

Barrett, who conclude that Paul is not the author, agree that the external

evidence supports Paul as author.
