Setting—Ephesus and Artemis

Ephesus was in the Roman province of Asia. Once Paul and Timothy

were forbidden to preach in the province of Asia by the Holy Spirit (Acts

16:6, c. A.D. 50), but eventually Ephesus became the home church for the

Apostle Paul’s mission for about three years (Acts 20:31, c. A.D. 54–56).

During these years he experienced marvels through God’s work and great

suering from persecution. He wrote 1 Corinthians from Ephesus (1 Cor
