City of Colossae

Colossae was a city in the Lycus Valley located within southwestern

Phrygia in the interior of Asia Minor. Colossae was once a densely

populated and wealthy city according to Xenophon, a city through which

Xerxes and his army passed in 480 BCE.

e Greek geographer Strabo
