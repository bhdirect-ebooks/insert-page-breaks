Proclaim the Word (4:1–6)

Paul then concludes by exhorting Timothy, in light of Jesus’ judgment, to

proclaim the word at all times because a time is coming when people will

turn from healthy teaching to myths (4:1–5). He gives a solemn warning:

I am charging, in the presence of God and Christ Jesus, the one com-

ing to judge the living and dead, and in his appearing and his kingdom:

proclaim the word, take a stand in season, out of season, reprove, re-

buke, encourage, in all compassion and teaching (4:1–2). Believers need

to mature spiritually (3:17) because they will face the judgment. Paul had

used the exact rst seven words in 1 Timothy 5:21 (I am charging in the

presence of God and Christ Jesus) in similar locations in each letter (about

one chapter before the close of the letter). He exhorts Timothy about a seri-
