2 TIMOTHY 3

Timothy Needs to Continue in Ministry despite Opposition

(3:1—4:8)

In this third major section (3:1—4:8) of the letter, Paul exhorts Timothy to

continue in his ministry of evangelism and sound teaching despite opposi-

tion, turning away (3:1–5) from those who lack genuine piety, opposing

the truth and taking women as prisoners. Eventually their folly will become

evident (3:6–9). Instead, Timothy needs to follow Paul’s model, expecting

the godly in Christ to be persecuted and evil people to be deceived (3:10–

13), while Timothy remains in the Scriptures, which prepare him for every

good work (3:14–17). Paul exhorts Timothy, in light of Jesus’ judgment,

to proclaim the word at all times because a time is coming when people

will turn from healthy teaching to myths and Paul will be gone (4:1–6), to

receive with all who love the Lord a just crown (4:7–8).
