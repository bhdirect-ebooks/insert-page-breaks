Paul, having mentioned his attitude to his imprisonment, the power of Je-

sus (1:12), the soundness and importance of Paul’s own words (1:12–13),

the receptiveness of Timothy (1:13), the presence of the Holy Spirit (1:14),

Paul has given many reasons how and why Timothy can continue to be

empowered. By using the pronoun you, Paul exhorts Timothy in particular

to be like Onesiphorus’ household and not like Phygelus and Hermogenes.

He calls Timothy again my child to remind him of their special spiritual

parent-child relationship.
