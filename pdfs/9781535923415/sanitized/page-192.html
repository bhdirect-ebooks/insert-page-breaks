Do Not Risk Siblings Stumbling (14:13–23)

Rather than “judging” others, Paul admonishes (playing on senses of

“judge,” cf. 14:10), one should “judge” not to provoke a fellow believer to

fall away from faith (14:13). Food is a secondary matter not worth risking

anyone’s salvation over (14:13, 15). For Paul, foods are neutral, neither

clean nor unclean (14:14; Paul is speaking ritually, according to Lev 11,

not equating all food hygienically or nutritionally).
