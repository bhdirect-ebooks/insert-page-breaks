Fusing the Horizons: Homosexual Activity

Scholars diverge fairly widely in their views about how to interpret Paul in

1:26–27, although a majority recognizes that Paul condemns homosexual be-

havior generally. Interpreters differ still more widely over how (and whether) to

apply Paul today. In view of this disparity, we need to understand the historical

context of Paul’s argument.
